ABOUT US

INTRODUCTION

SAMATVA is a nonprofit organization with dedicated team members who are committed to build an environment where every child feels safe and is equal regardless of their social class, ethnicity and background, where they enhance their foundation skills as well as learn some important moral values and norms.

Our mission

Our mission is to end the gap between high achieving and low achieving students making them feel equal and confident with what they have and whichever background they come from and leading their life with high moral values.Being an exceptional example in the community they come from.

We are committed to empower and provide employment opportunities to the motivated female youths of a community who are in verge of ending their higher studies, where they will rise themselves and lift the foundational education and enhance other aspects of the children living in the same community.

Objectives
To enhance the base of primary level students
Empowering the female youth who are struggling with low income status and finance for their higher education
To attract the students and parents towards public schools
